End Weight of Diluent Calculator

Description:

This is a desktop application that originally was created to calculate the amount of EtOH in a volume of H2O based solution. However, it was later adapted to the dilution of any solution with a known specific gravity. The GUI takes as input the diluents specific gravity, the desired percent (Vol/Vol) of the final solution, anad total desired volume of solution to be created. The output is the amount of all components of the solution to be created: The volume of H20 in mL, The volume of solute in mL, and the gram weights of solute and final solution.

3:2 Solution Calculator

Description:

This desktop application was originally designed for calculating an anesthetic mixture in our lab. Thus, it has the drug names to make the application clear for lab mates, however, it can be used for the calculation of any solution requiring a three to two parts mixture. The problem it solves is that one does not always want to mix exactly 3mL with 2mL of solution. Either to minimize the drug used or the experiment requires more total volume than 5mL. This app enables one to enter any desired final volume and get accurate component solutions. This eliminates human error as well as the arduous rigors of mathing.
